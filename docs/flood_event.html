Flood Event Observations

high-water marks from STN flood event data and high-water levels from gauges in USGS Water Data Services
Distribution

(left) In the high-water marks countplot, it's evident that Vermont has the largest number of observations, primarily from the 2023 July MA NY VT Flood event (547 observations). In contrast, Maine, the initially targeted state, has only 6 observations.

(right) In the high-water levels countplot, the 2023-07 event in Vermont has the largest number of observations (20) across all states. High-water levels help fill gaps in the data. Some states affected by specific events are not reflected in the high-water marks but captured in the high-water levels.
